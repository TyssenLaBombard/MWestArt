Mike West Art

Who Is Mike West?

Hello, my name is Michael West a game design major at Fitchburg state.
Most of my work primarily revolves around creating models,concepts,
and designs, for game characters. In my free time I like to
hangout with friends, work on personal projects, play guitar,
and read. I started drawing seriously around 13 years old.

Pixel Art History

I started doing basic forms of pixel art around my sophomore year of high school.
Mostly just some ideas and cool things that I learned how to do on my own.
Some of my earliest concepts led me to different interests in terms of the various models I liked creating.
Currently almost all games I make or create ideas for are pixel art based.

Life Drawing History

I only started life drawing this past semester, but I have grown to love it a lot.
Though it was intimidating working with nude models to start the lessons it taught me in
working with pastels and different shading has been tremendous. The work in life drawing
does not directly relate to my primary work in pixel art but it does make me a better artist overall.

3D Renders History

I started doing 3D renders during my senior year of high school. After working
with pixel art for two years I got a found some software that could render and assist
in modeling 3D figures and designs. This gave way to a whole new avenue for me and was a
lot of fun learning how to do.
